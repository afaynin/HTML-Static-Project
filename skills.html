Benefits of Juggling

As we all know, the job market is tough out there, and if you really want to succeed in life you better be a good juggler. Interviewers are always talking about juggling this weird thing called ‘responsibilities’, but until you learn to do that you can at least show the interviewer that at least you can juggle some balls. The following are some of the benefits:

  • You can say that you know how to juggle
  • You know how to juggle
  • You can teach others how to juggle

Learning how to juggle

To learn how to juggle, all you need to have is three of the same object that you could reasonably throw over your head. Now, hold all three of them, and then throw one of those objects over your head, and while it is at the apex of the throw, throw another object, and than catch the intitally thrown object. Repeat all previous steps except the first one for the rest of eternity and BAM you are now juggling!
