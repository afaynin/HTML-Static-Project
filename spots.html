The Best Places in NYC

The Food Places

Joes Pizza

An absolutely famous joint in Manhattan with some of the most famous celebrities having eaten their at least once, this is a must visit.

Hometown Bar-B-Que

New York may not be known for its BBQ, but this establishment in Brooklyn is one of the best, and is an essential visit if you're craving some sauce soaked meat.

The Sights

Statue of Liberty

While this monument is a little too close to New Jersey for comfort, it still represents one of the most famous statues in the world as well as a symbol of America
